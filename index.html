The Worlds Best Spaghetti With Fresh Sauce

By Ryan Rhodes

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Fresh Cherry Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Olive Oil
  • Square Spaghetti Noodles (normal will also work)

Instructions

  1. Heat up a pan to medium heat with olive oil
  2. Cut cherry tomotoes into halves
  3. Dice the garlic and add to the pan once heated, heat for about 30 second to a minute
  4. Once the garlic has been heated up add in the halved tomatoes
  5. While the tomatoes are cooking, add your pasta to boiling water and cook to al dente per box
  6. Cook until the tomatoes have released all their juice
  7. Once the tomatoes are done, add in a few leaves of fresh basil
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the pan with some pasta water
  9. Mix and enjoy with freshly grated parmesan

This is my favorite recipe as the sauce from fresh tomatoes has a very light taste which I find delicious in the summer months. It is not to overpowering and strong, and is best enjoyed on a nice summer evening with a glass of red wine!
